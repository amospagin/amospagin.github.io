Adding proper punctuation after page indexes in BibLatex

2020-08-25

I’m not sure why, but the standard implementation of the APA-format for BibLatex kept messing up the punctuation after page indexes in the bibliography. To solve this, I added the following to the tex-file:

\DeclareFieldFormat[article]{pages}{#1\addperiod}

It seemed to do the trick.
