PhD beginnings

2020-08-25

After a long, strange summer (note to future me: remember COVID-19?) I’m finally starting as a PhD student at the University of Gothenburg. It’s an interesting time to do a PhD — working at the office is strongly discouraged, so I’m stuck at home struggling to find a comfortable typing position in my bed (the room I’m renting is unfurnished). Perhaps I’ll make some physiotherapist very rich in the future.

Anyway, work on the dissertation will presumably start tomorrow. Which reminds me that I should probably go out and buy more coffee.
