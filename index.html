I'm a PhD student in psychology at the University of Gothenburg, where I investigate how adults (and their brains) learn reasoning and abstract problem-solving skills. I’m also a member of the Lövdén Lab.

Before this, I obtained a master’s degree in psychology at Uppsala University in 2020, and a bachelor’s degree in psychology (with minors in theoretical philosophy and linguistics) at Stockholm University in 2018. For more information, see my CV.
