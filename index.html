Short Bio
I’m a PhD student in psychology at the University of Gothenburg, where I investigate how adults (and their brains) learn to reason and solve abstract problems. I'm also part of the Lövdén Lab. (→ Read more about my research here.)

Before this, I obtained a master’s degree in psychology at Uppsala University in 2020, and a bachelor’s degree in psychology (with minors in theoretical philosophy and linguistics) at Stockholm University in 2018.
